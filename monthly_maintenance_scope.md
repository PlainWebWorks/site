# What Monthly Maintenance Covers

Monthly maintenance is for keeping the basic web presence working, current, and easy to trust. It is not meant to be a mystery fee or a retainer for unlimited redesign work.

## Included

### Small Website Updates

- Text changes
- Hours, phone, email, or address updates
- Service list updates
- Staff or bio updates
- Swapping photos the business provides
- Small layout adjustments when needed

The goal is to handle normal business changes without making the owner touch the website system.

### Basic Health Checks

- Check that the site loads
- Check that contact forms still work
- Check important links
- Check mobile layout for obvious issues
- Check SSL/security certificate status
- Check for broken pages or missing images

These checks are meant to catch the simple problems that quietly cost small businesses leads.

### Contact Form And Lead Flow

- Confirm form submissions are reaching the right inbox
- Update form recipients when staff or email changes
- Review spam problems when they show up
- Adjust simple form fields when needed

A website is only useful if customers can actually reach the business.

### Domain And DNS Guidance

- Keep track of where the domain is registered
- Help avoid accidental domain expiration
- Review DNS records before changes are made
- Help with basic domain-related troubleshooting

The business keeps ownership of the domain and accounts. Maintenance helps keep those pieces understandable.

### Email Deliverability Basics

- Review SPF, DKIM, and DMARC records when email providers change
- Help troubleshoot obvious delivery problems
- Keep business email connected to the domain setup

This does not include managing every mailbox or acting as full IT support, but it does cover the records that affect whether business email looks legitimate.

### Google Business Profile Support

- Help update website, phone, hours, and service information
- Help maintain a review link
- Review basic profile details for consistency
- Advise on verification or listing issues

Google controls approval, verification, ranking, and placement. Maintenance covers practical support and cleanup, not guaranteed search position.

### Analytics And Search Console Checks

- Confirm analytics is still installed
- Confirm Search Console access where possible
- Watch for obvious indexing or crawl warnings
- Provide plain-English notes when something needs attention

This is basic visibility monitoring, not advanced SEO reporting.

### Basic Security Hygiene

- Encourage MFA on important accounts
- Help identify mystery logins and account ownership issues
- Keep a simple list of key services where possible
- Advise before risky account or DNS changes

The goal is to prevent common small-business problems: lost domains, inaccessible accounts, broken email, and vendor lock-in.

## Not Included

- Full redesigns
- New multi-page site builds
- Logo design or full branding packages
- Custom software development
- Full ad campaign management
- Social media posting
- Writing large amounts of new copy every month
- Managing employee computers, printers, or office networks
- Emergency support for issues caused by third-party outages
- Guaranteed Google rankings, leads, reviews, or sales

Work outside the monthly scope can still be quoted separately.

## Simple Webpage Version

Monthly maintenance covers small edits, form checks, broken link checks, domain and DNS guidance, email record support, Google Business Profile updates, analytics/Search Console checks, and basic security hygiene. It does not include full redesigns, ad management, social media posting, custom software, or guaranteed search rankings.
