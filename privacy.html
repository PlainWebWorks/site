Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

The short version

We do not collect personal information for marketing purposes. We do not sell data. We do not have a user account system. Visiting this website does not give us your personal information.

What we collect and why

Contact inquiries

When you click a contact or email link on this site, your email client opens and you choose whether to send a message. If you do send an email, we receive your email address and the contents of your message. We use that information only to respond to your inquiry. We do not add you to any mailing list, share your information with third parties, or use it for any purpose other than responding to you.

Analytics

This site uses Cloudflare Web Analytics, which collects anonymous, aggregate data — things like page views, general geographic region, and device type. It does not use cookies, does not track you across websites, and does not collect personal information. We use this data to understand whether the site is functioning and how it is being used in general terms.

Hosting and infrastructure

This site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may log standard server data such as IP addresses and browser information as part of normal infrastructure operation. This is governed by Cloudflare's privacy policy.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information.
  • We do not run advertising or tracking pixels.
  • We do not operate a newsletter or email list.
  • We do not collect payment information on this website.

Contact and deletion requests

If you have contacted us and want your information removed, email hello@plainwebworks.co and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a meaningful way, we will update the date at the top of this page.
