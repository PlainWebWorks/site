## FAQ

### Do I need a new website?

Maybe not. We start by checking what you already have. If it can be fixed, we’ll tell you. If it needs replacing, we’ll tell you that too. No fake emergency.

### Can you fix my current website?

Usually, yes. If the current site is salvageable, we can clean up broken forms, outdated pages, basic search settings, domain records, email setup, and account access. If your current platform blocks something, we’ll say so.

### Do you replace Wix, Squarespace, GoDaddy, or vendor systems?

Only when there is a good reason. A lot of businesses do not need a full rebuild. They need the mess around the existing setup cleaned up.

### Can you help with my Google Business listing?

Yes. We can help set up or clean up your Google Business Profile, connect your website, add key details, check services/categories, and create a review link. Google controls verification and placement. We handle the setup.

### Can you set up business email?

Yes. We can set up professional email like name@yourbusiness.com and configure the domain records that help mail providers trust your messages. That includes SPF, DKIM, and DMARC.

### Do you run ads?

Not as part of the normal setup packages. We can prepare your site for ads by making sure the landing page, tracking, and contact flow are sane. Full ad campaign management is separate.

### What do I own after setup?

You own your domain, your accounts, your website content, and access to your finished site files where possible. We do not build hostage websites.

### Do you work outside the local area?

Yes. We are starting with local Georgia businesses, but most of this work can be done remotely for small businesses anywhere in the US.
